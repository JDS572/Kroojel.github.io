Manual Testing

Manual testing workflow:

  • Boot up game, ensure it opens
  • Move ship to ground cell, ensure it doesnt pass through
  • Before collecting any powerups, open shop. Ensure that:
    • Shop window opens
    • Buttons and progress bars scale correctly
    • Purchase is impossible
  • Ensure ally ship follows player
  • Shoot at an npc ship, a college defender, an ally ship. Ensure that:
    • NPC takes damage and renders healthbars
    • ally does not take damage
    • college defender take damage and renders healthbar
  • If current quest is a chest quest, collect and ensure plunder received
  • Ensure bad weather animation renders and fades in and out
  • If over 50 plunder collected, open shop. Ensure that:
    • Shop window opens
    • Buttons and progress bars scale correctly
    • Purchase is possible
  • Exit shop and enter pause menu. Ensure it is scaled and renders correctly. Save. Go to main menu.
  • From the main menu, load the game, ensure game spawns and the position of the ship as well as the starts match those from before saving.
